About Taiwan Hikes

The content, images (unless specified) and code are mainly written or taken by Anusha Lee. Anusha is an avid hiker and a web developer to be. She starts hiking regularly in early 2017 and has been learning how to code since early 2018.

Why this website?

There are so many hiking clubs in Taiwan and as a new hiker, it was a no-brainer for Anusha to hike with them. On those hiking trips, she met many hikers who also share the love to the nature in Taiwan.

There are many benefits to hike with groups in Taiwan, and the biggest ones are safety and convenience. Basically, you don’t have to worry about how to navigate on the trails as long as you stay close to your hiking guides or mates.

While on the trails, Anusha met hikers from different countries, and they all share something in common. They love to go hiking with the locals during their stay in Taiwan, but the information in English is limited. Anusha even heard terrible stories about how visitors chose the wrong trails, ended up getting lost in the woods and were forced to stay there overnight without proper gear.

Since Anusha was looking for personal projects to showcase her coding skills, she thought why didn’t she combine those things she enjoys together? That’s how Taiwan Hikes was born. Furthermore, she can also promote the nature and hiking in Taiwan.

This is an ongoing project and Anusha will update the content of Taiwan Hikes from time to time. If you’d like to know more about Anusha’s journey of code learning, please click here.
